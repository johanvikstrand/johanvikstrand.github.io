About Me

I am 23 years old and originally come from Lidköping, Västergötland. I currently live in Lund together with my girlfriend, Gabriela, and her dog. I come from a family prone of technical interest since both my father and brother work as Automation Engineers and my mother also works in a technical field. From an early age I was active in a range of different sports, which I believe have been a crucial part in why I like to work in groups and cooperate towards a common goal.

I believe that time spent improving on a skill or a craft is time well spent. I am eager to learn, to understand how a high technology organization work and to put in practice the learnings that I have acquired so far.
